\chapter*{Logistics}\label{logistics}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Logistics}

Welcome to \textbf{EE BIOL C234: Practical Computing for Evolutionary
Biologists and Ecologists}---or as I like to call it, ``21st century
Kung-Fu!''

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  \textbf{Where and When}: We'll be meeting Mondays and Wednesdays,
  11:00 AM to 11:50 AM, in Botany 108.
\item
  \textbf{The Grind}: Yes, there will be weekly assignments. If you want
  to build computational muscle, you're going to have to sweat a bit.
  But rest easy, no terrifying final exam to ruin your holidays!
\item
  \textbf{What to Bring}: Your laptop and, more importantly, your
  intellectual curiosity.
\item
  \textbf{Course Material}: This lecture note will be updated as the
  course progresses.
\end{itemize}

This is the inaugural run of this course for me. So if I'm losing you,
boring you, talking too fast, too slow, whatever -- \emph{speak up}!
Your feedback is my compass.
